The New Kid On The Block

I work with sending out hardware, some coding, handling calls, and troubleshooting issues with both hardware and computers. My day to day schedule looks like the following: Start with a meeting, configure some hardware for a few hours, take a break and walk around the building to help out with any computer issues, break for lunch, drop my configured hardware off for shipping, perform some troubleshooting for off site locations, and head home.

When I am not working, I'm usually at home with my dogs. During the first couple hours, I tend to let my dogs out, work on homework, and clean up after work. Whenever my family comes home, I spend time with them before we eat dinner and talk about our days. Once dinner is over, I usually head upstairs to play a bit of games with my friends to catch up with them before I hop off and go to bed.
